Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this app?
This app aims to promote transparency and accountability by providing detailed information about each county in Kenya, including their governors, senators, women representatives, population, area, and capital.
How can I search for information about a specific county?
You can search for information by using the search bar at the top of the app. Simply type in the name of the county, and the relevant information will be displayed.
How often is the data updated?
The data is updated regularly to ensure accuracy and reflect any changes in the county officials or other key information.
Where does the data come from?
The data is sourced from official government publications and verified databases to ensure accuracy and reliability.
